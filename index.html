Meet Kathryn

The Kathryn Standard grew out of the conversations women kept having in hallways, DMs and side chats — about what actually works, what’s marketing, and how to hold a higher standard for our faces, our health and our work.

Kathryn works at the intersection of aesthetics, peptides, supplements and business. She has spent years inside beauty and wellness spaces — consulting, testing, listening — and this site is where she distills those experiences into something quieter and more useful.

This isn’t a place to collect products. It’s a place to collect language, frameworks and standards so you can make better decisions with your practitioners, your purchases and your business.
